FCSys.Subregions.Species.H2O.Liquid.Fixed

Fixed properties

Information

Assumptions:

  1. The generalized resistivities (β, ζ, θ) are fixed (e.g., independent of temperature).

Notes:

  • The specific heat capacity is not fixed because it would affect the chemical potential and result in an incorrect cell potential.

The default resistivities (zeta=1/(855e-6*U.Pa*U.s) and theta=U.m*U.K/(613e-3*U.W)) are of H2O liquid at saturation pressure and 300 K from Incropera and DeWitt [Table 1 lists the properties at saturation pressure and other temperatures. See also http://www.engineeringtoolbox.com/water-dynamic-kinematic-viscosity-d_596.html.
