FCSys.Characteristics.'H+'.Solid

H+ in solid

Information

The enthalpy of formation (Deltah0_f) and the additional enthalpy offset (h_offset) are specified such that the enthalpy in the Species model is referenced to zero at 0 °C. Otherwise, there is a very large bias that causes problems with the reaction. The proper values are not known from the NASA CEA thermodynamic data [McBride2002] (it only provides data for H+ gas). Since the same H+ model is used in the anode and cathode, there is no net effect.

The specific volume of protons corresponds to the concentration measured by Spry and Fayer (0.95 M) in Nafion® at λ = 12, where λ is the number of H2O molecules to SO3H endgroups. At λ = 22, the concentration was measured at 0.54 M [Spry2009].
