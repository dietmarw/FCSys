FCSys.Subregions.Species.O2.Gas.Fixed

Fixed properties

Information

Assumptions:

  1. Ideal gas
  2. The generalized resistivities (β, ζ, θ) are fixed (e.g., independent of temperature).

Additional notes:

  • The specific heat capacity is not fixed because it would affect the chemical potential and result in an incorrect cell potential.

The default resistivities (zeta=1/(207.2e-7*U.Pa*U.s) and theta=U.m*U.K/(26.8e-3*U.W)) are based on data of gas at 1 atm and 300 K from Incropera and DeWitt [Incropera2002, pp. 920–921]. Table 2 lists values of the material resistivity or self diffusion coefficient.
