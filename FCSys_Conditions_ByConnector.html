FCSys.Conditions.ByConnector

Conditions for each type of connector

Information

This package contains models to impose conditions on each of the declarative connectors established in FCSys.Connectors. The subpackages are named according to the corresponding connector.

Extends from Modelica.Icons.Package (Icon for standard packages).

Package Content

NameDescription
 Reaction Conditions for a Reaction connector
 Chemical Conditions for a Chemical connector
 PhysicalBus Conditions for a PhysicalBus or PhysicalBusInternal connector
 Physical Conditions for a Physical connector
 FaceBus Conditions for the FaceBus connector
 Face Conditions for the Face connector
 Inert Conditions for an Inert connector
 Amagat Conditions for a Amagat connector
 Dalton Conditions for a Dalton connector
 Translational Conditions for a Translational connector
 ThermalDiffusion Conditions for a ThermalDiffusion connector
