Modelica definition

model Calibrated "Correlations with adjustment factors"
  extends Species(
    redeclare replaceable package Data = FCSys.Characteristics.H2O.Gas (b_v=[1],
          n_v={-1,0}),
    beta=k_beta*Data.beta(T, v),
    zeta=k_zeta*Data.zeta(T, v),
    theta=k_theta*Data.theta(T, v));

  parameter Q.NumberAbsolute k_beta(final nominal=1) = 1 
    "Adjustment factor for dynamic compressibility (kβ)";
  parameter Q.NumberAbsolute k_zeta(final nominal=1) = 1 
    "Adjustment factor for fluidity (kζ)";
  parameter Q.NumberAbsolute k_theta(final nominal=1) = 1 
    "Adjustment factor for thermal resistivity (kθ)";

end Calibrated;

FCSys.Subregions.Species.H2O.Gas.Fixed

Fixed properties

Information

Assumptions:

  1. Ideal gas
  2. The generalized resistivities (β, ζ, θ) are fixed (e.g., independent of temperature).

Notes:

  • The specific heat capacity is not fixed because it would affect the chemical potential and result in an incorrect cell potential.

The default resistivities (zeta=1/(9.09e-6*U.Pa*U.s) and theta=U.m*U.K/(19.6e-3*U.W)) are of H2O gas at saturation pressure and 300 K from Incropera and DeWitt [Table 1 lists the properties at saturation pressure and other temperatures. Table 2 lists the properties of H2O gas at 1 atm. See also http://www.engineeringtoolbox.com/water-dynamic-kinematic-viscosity-d_596.html.
