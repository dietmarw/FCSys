FCSys.Characteristics.O2.Gas

O2 gas

Information

Notes:

  • According to [Avogadro1.03], the (center-to-center) bond length of O-O is 128.2 pm. The radius of O is from http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxygen. See also http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Van_der_Waals_radius.
  • The virial coefficients are from Dymond et al. (2002, p. 69). The temperature range of the coefficients is [70, 495] K, but this is not enforced in the functions.

For more information, see the Characteristic record.

Extends from BaseClasses.CharacteristicNASA (Thermodynamic record with transport properties based on NASA CEA).
