FCSys.Connectors.BaseClasses.PartialChemical

Partial connector to exchange material while advecting linear momentum and enthalpy

Information

Current is positive into the component. Note that this is different than the Face connector, where areic current is positive in the globally positive direction.

For more information, see the documentation in the Connectors package.

Extends from FCSys.Connectors.Mechanical (Connector to exchange or transport linear momentum).
